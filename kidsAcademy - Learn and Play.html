Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

Kids Academy - Learn and Play is designed for children and families. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children. This app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable data protection regulations.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Kids Academy - Learn and Play application (the "App") for mobile devices, developed and published by FiFODALE (SMC-PRIVATE) LIMITED ("we," "us," or "our"). The App is provided as an ad-supported service with optional in-app purchases to remove advertisements.

Information We Collect

We designed Kids Academy with children's privacy in mind. Here's what you need to know:

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses from users
  • We do not collect precise geolocation data
  • We do not require user registration or account creation
  • We do not collect photos, videos, or audio recordings from users
  • We do not use analytics that track individual user behavior

Information That May Be Collected Automatically

The App uses third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) which may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID) for serving contextual, non-personalized advertisements
  • General device information (device type, operating system version) for app compatibility
  • App interaction data for ad frequency capping

Important: We have configured our advertising services to serve only child-appropriate, non-personalized advertisements in compliance with COPPA regulations.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Apple App Store (for iOS users)

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies. We have implemented settings to ensure child-safe advertising practices.

Advertising

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Here's what you need to know:

Child-Safe Advertising Practices

  • Parental Verification Required: A parental gate (math verification) must be passed before ANY advertisement is displayed. Children cannot view ads without parental consent.
  • COPPA-Compliant Ads Only: We use Google AdMob's child-directed treatment setting, ensuring only COPPA-compliant advertisements are served.
  • Non-Personalized Ads: We do not serve behaviorally targeted or interest-based ads to users.
  • Age-Appropriate Content: Ad content rating is restricted to "G" (General Audiences). Google AdMob reviews ad creatives through both automated systems and human review to ensure appropriateness for children.
  • No Deceptive Ads: Advertisements are clearly distinguishable from app content.

Information Collected by Advertising Services

Google AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (for ad frequency capping only)
  • General device information (device type, OS version)
  • App interaction data (for limiting ad repetition)

This data is NOT used for personalized advertising or user tracking. For more information, see Google AdMob's Child-Directed Treatment policy.

Opting Out of Ads

Parents can remove all advertisements by subscribing to our ad-free plan, which is protected by parental verification.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional subscription purchases to remove advertisements. These purchases are processed entirely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not directly collect or store any payment information. All transactions are subject to the respective store's privacy policy and parental controls.

A parental verification gate is implemented before any purchase can be made to prevent unauthorized transactions by children.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Kids Academy is designed for use by children. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age
  • The App does not contain features that allow children to share personal information publicly
  • Advertisements displayed are configured for child audiences (COPPA-compliant, non-personalized ads)
  • The App includes parental gates to protect access to external links, settings, and in-app purchases
  • We do not engage in behavioral advertising targeting children

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at info@fifodale.com. We will take steps to delete such information from our systems promptly.

Parental Controls

We encourage parents and guardians to:

  • Supervise their children's use of the App
  • Use device-level parental controls available on iOS and Android devices
  • Configure purchase restrictions through your device's app store settings
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns about the App's content or privacy practices

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data retained on our servers. Any locally stored app preferences (such as sound settings) are stored only on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of any information processed through the App. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of personalized advertising: You can reset your device's advertising identifier or enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in your device settings
  • Delete local data: Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored preferences
  • Request information: Contact us to inquire about any data practices

International Users

The App may be used by users worldwide. By using the App, you consent to the processing of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of your location.
